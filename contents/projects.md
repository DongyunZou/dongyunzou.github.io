#### Kernel Design Agents (KDA)

- Agentic CUDA kernel optimization workflow for the <strong>MLSys 2026 FlashInfer AI Kernel Generation Contest</strong>. As part of HAN Lab Kernel Mafia, we ranked <strong>1st</strong> in Fused MoE, <strong>2nd</strong> in Sparse Attention (DSA), and <strong>3rd</strong> in Gated Delta Net (GDN) in the Full-Agent track.
